# Code of Conduct – IBM Full Stack Software Developer Certificate

Welcome to the learning community! This Code of Conduct outlines our shared expectations for creating a respectful, inclusive, and productive environment for everyone in the IBM Full Stack Software Developer Certificate course.

---

## Our Pledge

We—participants, learners, and contributors—pledge to make this course a positive, welcoming space for all. We’re here to learn together, no matter your background, experience, or identity.

---

## Our Standards

We believe great learning happens when everyone feels safe and respected. Here’s what that looks like in practice:

### Encouraged Behaviors
- Using respectful and inclusive language
- Valuing different perspectives and experiences
- Offering supportive and constructive feedback
- Staying focused on shared learning goals
- Showing empathy and understanding to others

### Unacceptable Behaviors
- Harassment, discrimination, or bullying in any form
- Disrespectful or disruptive communication
- Plagiarism or dishonest academic conduct
- Violating Coursera’s terms or academic policies
- Anything that harms the learning experience for others

---

## Our Responsibilities

### Instructors & Facilitators
Course leaders are here to support your learning journey. They’ll provide guidance, address concerns fairly, and help maintain a positive environment.

### All Participants
You play a key role too! Please:
- Treat others with kindness and respect
- Submit your own original work
- Speak up if you see inappropriate behavior
- Help build a collaborative and supportive community

---

## Enforcement

If something doesn’t feel right, please report it to the course instructors or administrators. All reports will be reviewed, and appropriate actions—such as a warning, restricted access, or escalation to Coursera—may be taken depending on the situation.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all course-related spaces: discussions, assignments, projects, and any other activities tied to the IBM Full Stack Software Developer Certificate.
